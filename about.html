Biography

My story begins in September 1988 in the rainy city of Portland, OR. Originally, I received a Bachelors’ degree in Business Management from Oregon State University, and had received my first job with Apple Inc working with their online-based sales team. My employment came at the height of the debut of innovative devices like the iPad and their new Retina-display enabled devices, which proved excellent timing for my growing interests in art and design.

As I was being exposed to these devices on a regular basis, as well as customizing solutions for design professionals, I gained a greater interest of the many tools available to artists and designers of all levels. It was so much so, that I began doing freelance artwork and logo design for local businesses, with many of my skills inherited by self-teaching.

Desiring to grow further in my artistic abilities on the internet, I enrolled in Bloc.io's Designer Apprenticeship program to inherit these needed skills. With the guidance of my talented mentors, I gained greater knowledge of design aesthetic and color theory, as well as the most important concepts in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. With this newfound knowledge, I was ready to make a career change, and departed from Apple to fully pursue my amibitions of becoming a full-time Website and UI Designer.

It’s been over seven years that I have been cultivating my skills in both art and design, and I find that my desire for growing as a designer still thrives and the field of Website and UI Design is no longer just a field I want to pursue for employment, but is a field that has become one of my greatest passions.

“Choose a job you love, and you will never have to work a day in your life…” - Confucius

My Favorite Hobbies

When not behind my workstation, you can often find me on weekends in the North End of Providence Park cheering on the Portland Timbers Major League Soccer team. I am a season-ticket holder, and enjoy the thrill and energy that comes from the beautiful game.

I am also an avid fan-artist and writer. What does that mean exactly? Well, if there's a Television, Anime, or Video Game character or scene that captures my interest, I can't help but get inspired to draw or write it out. I often find it cathartic and a great way to keep my skills sharpened as an artist and writer.

But of course, I am always acquiring new hobbies each day, because who doesn't love trying new things?
